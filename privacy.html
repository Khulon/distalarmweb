Privacy Policy for DistAlarm

Effective Date: July 15, 2025

DistAlarm does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your location data is used only on your device to trigger alarms based on your chosen radius and is never transmitted or stored externally.

We do not:

  • Collect or store your personal information
  • Track your location in the background unnecessarily
  • Use analytics or advertising frameworks

Permissions:

DistAlarm uses location services to detect when you enter or exit your chosen area. It also uses notifications and alarm sounds to alert you. These permissions are only active when the app is in use or running a saved alarm.

Contact:

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us via our support form.

By using DistAlarm, you agree to this privacy policy.
