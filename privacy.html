Privacy Policy for DistAlarm

Effective Date: September 22, 2025

DistAlarm respects your privacy. We are committed to being transparent about what information is collected and how it is used.

Information We Collect

DistAlarm itself does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your location data is used only on your device to trigger alarms based on your chosen radius and is never transmitted or stored externally.

Advertising

DistAlarm uses Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect certain information, such as device identifiers, usage data, and approximate location, to provide personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your settings.

You can manage your ad preferences, including limiting ad tracking, in your device’s settings.

For more details, please review Google’s Advertising Policy.

We do not:

  • Collect or store your personal information ourselves
  • Track your location in the background unnecessarily
  • Sell your data to third parties

Permissions

DistAlarm uses location services to detect when you enter or exit your chosen area. It also uses notifications and alarm sounds to alert you. These permissions are only active when the app is in use or running a saved alarm.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us via our support form.

By using DistAlarm, you agree to this privacy policy.
